Privacy

Simple privacy for a free testing-stage brief.

AI Signal Desk is in a free beta. This page explains, in plain terms, what is collected, how it is used, and the promises that come with it.

What is collected

If you join the free beta, AI Signal Desk may collect your email address and any optional answers you submit about your role, interests, or the AI topics you want filtered.

Who processes your email

Signups are handled by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), a third-party email service, which stores your address and sends the field brief on AI Signal Desk's behalf. Joining uses double opt-in: nothing is sent until you confirm through the email Kit sends you. See Kit's privacy policy for how they handle your data.

How it is used

  • To send beta updates or sample weekly briefs.
  • To understand what kinds of AI signals readers want more of.
  • To improve the editorial format before choosing paid tooling.

No selling

AI Signal Desk will not sell your email address or beta feedback. The beta exists to test whether the field brief is useful, nothing more.

Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email from the field brief, or by contacting the address on the contact page.

Analytics

AI Signal Desk uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to see basic traffic, like which pages are read most. It is privacy-first and cookieless: no cookies are set, no personal data is collected, and there is no cross-site tracking or advertising. See Cloudflare Web Analytics for how it works.
