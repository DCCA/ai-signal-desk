Privacy

Simple privacy for a free testing-stage brief.

What is collected

If you join the free beta, AI Signal Desk may collect your email address and any optional answers you submit about your role, interests, or AI topics you want filtered.

How it is used

  • To send beta updates or sample weekly briefs.
  • To understand what kinds of AI signals readers want more of.
  • To improve the editorial format before choosing paid tooling.

No selling

AI Signal Desk will not sell your email address or beta feedback. The beta is for testing whether the field brief is useful.

Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe or ask to be removed at any time by using the reply path in a beta email or contacting the address on the contact page.
