About the desk

A calm intelligence desk for the AI firehose.

AI Signal Desk filters the daily flood of AI news into a small set of things actually worth your attention. Less newsletter, more operations desk: every update is classified by usefulness, scored for signal versus hype, and given a concrete next action.

01

Classify

Every update is sorted into concept, product, repo, or workflow, so you always know what kind of thing you are looking at.

02

Score

Each item gets a signal score and a hype score from 0 to 100, making editorial judgment legible instead of implied.

03

Action

Nothing ships without a verdict: learn, try, watch, or ignore, plus one concrete thing to do next.

What we hold to.

01
Signal over volume
We classify by usefulness, not trend velocity. Fewer items, chosen well.
02
Every item gets an action
No tool lists without a next step. If we can't say what to do, we don't run it.
03
The hype filter is the product
We tell you what to ignore as clearly as what to try.
04
Machine-assisted, human-judged
Drafting is automated; the editorial call is reviewed by a human before it ships.
