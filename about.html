About the desk

Practical AI signal reports, not launch-chasing.

Editorial standard

AI Signal Desk is a free testing-stage field brief for builders, operators, PMs, and curious professionals who want to understand the AI updates that are actually worth attention.

Every useful signal should answer: what changed, why it matters, what to try next, and whether to learn, try, watch, or ignore it.

What gets included

  • Durable concepts that make AI systems easier to understand.
  • Products with a concrete use case and practical tradeoffs.
  • Repos with useful patterns, real adoption, or strong learning value.
  • Workflows a reader can copy, test, or adapt this week.

What gets ignored

  • Pure launch announcements with no practical takeaway.
  • Generic AI tool lists without judgment.
  • Breathless “everything changed” claims.
  • Items that are interesting but not useful yet.

Human review promise

Hermes may help filter newsletters, draft cards, and organize research, but public items should stay human review gated. The goal is better judgment, not automated publishing.
