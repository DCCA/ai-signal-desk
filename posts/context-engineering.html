Concept / Learn

Context engineering

What changed

The bottleneck in many AI workflows is whether the system provides the right instructions, files, examples, tool results, memory, and constraints at the right time.

Why it matters

Good context turns a model call into a reusable workflow. Bad context creates drift, stale assumptions, token waste, and confident but ungrounded output.

Try this

  1. Write a one-page operating brief for a recurring task.
  2. Separate stable rules from per-run inputs.
  3. Add sections for evidence and non-goals.

Signal judgment

Status: Learn. Confidence: High. Hype risk: low when tied to real workflow artifacts.
